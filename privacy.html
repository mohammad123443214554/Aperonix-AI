Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At Aperonix AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our AI chat service. Please read this policy carefully. By using Aperonix AI, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Aperonix AI, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Chat Data: Messages and interactions are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform (via analytics tools).
  • Technical Data: Browser type, operating system, IP address, device information.
  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device to improve your experience and serve relevant ads.
  • Contact Form Data: Name and email address submitted voluntarily through our contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Aperonix AI service
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our features and content
  • Understand and analyze how users interact with our platform
  • Communicate with you, including customer support and updates
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense and similar services
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Aperonix AI uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality (e.g., theme preferences, session data).
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our platform anonymously.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display personalized advertisements based on your interests and browsing history.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the platform. By continuing to use Aperonix AI, you consent to our use of cookies.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

Aperonix AI may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense and other third-party advertising partners. These ad networks use cookies and web beacons to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites across the internet
  • Measure advertisement effectiveness and track conversions
  • Personalize the advertisements you see based on your interests and browsing behavior

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on visits to Aperonix AI and other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze information about how visitors use our platform. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior data in an anonymous form. The information generated is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your chat history is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data you share with us, including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and secure API communication with Google Gemini. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Services

Aperonix AI integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Aperonix AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can delete such information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our Contact page.
